How to Sign PDF Online?

The PDF Mechanic allows you to electronically sign documents without printing or scanning. Our eSign PDF tool is fast, free, and secure.

  • Draw Signature: Use your mouse or finger to draw a signature.
  • Upload Image: Use a scanned image of your signature.
  • Secure: Files are processed locally on your device.

Steps to Sign

  1. Upload your PDF file.
  2. Click Create Signature to draw.
  3. Drag the signature to the correct position.
  4. Click Download PDF.
