PDF to Word Converter

This tool extracts textual content from your PDF and places it into a Word (.docx) document with basic line grouping and page breaks.

  • Text-first: Great for documents with selectable text.
  • Client-side: Runs entirely in your browser.
  • Fast & Free: No registration, no uploads.

Note: Complex layouts, tables, and images are not preserved. Scanned PDFs without selectable text require OCR, which is not included.

FAQ

Does it keep page breaks?

Yes. We insert Word page breaks between PDF pages.

Can it convert scanned PDFs?

Not yet. Scanned PDFs need OCR to turn images into text. This tool works on PDFs with selectable text.
