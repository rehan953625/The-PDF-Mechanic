How to Compress Images for Government Forms (SSC, UPSC)

Are you trying to upload a photo for an online application but facing the error "File size must be between 20kb and 50kb"? You are not alone. Most government portals in India, including SSC, UPSC, and IBPS, have strict size limits for uploading signatures and photographs.

The PDF Mechanic Image Compressor helps you resize your photos instantly without uploading them to a risky server. Since the processing happens Client-Side (in your browser), your personal photos never leave your mobile or laptop.

Why use this Client-Side Image Tool?

  • 100% Privacy: Unlike other websites, we don't store your photos. Everything happens on your device.
  • No Quality Loss: We use advanced algorithms to reduce file size while keeping the image sharp.
  • Works Offline: Once the page loads, you can turn off the internet and still compress images.
  • Unlimited Free Use: Resize as many photos as you want without any hidden costs.

Steps to Reduce Image Size to 20KB

  1. Click on the "Choose Image File" button above.
  2. Select your JPG or PNG photo from your gallery.
  3. Use the Quality Slider to adjust the file size.
    Tip: Slide it down to 50% or 40% to reach under 20KB.
  4. Watch the "Compressed Size" indicator update in real-time.
  5. Click Download once you are happy with the size.
