Why compress PDF to 100kb?

Many government websites (like SSC, UPSC, IBPS) and university portals have strict upload limits. Large files are rejected immediately. Using The PDF Mechanic, you can resize PDF to 100kb easily while keeping the text readable for verification purposes.

Is the compression quality good?

Yes. We use advanced algorithms to remove unnecessary metadata and optimize embedded images. This reduces file size drastically without making the document blurry.
