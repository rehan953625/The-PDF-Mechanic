Frequently Asked Questions

Is it safe to upload sensitive docs?

Yes. Unlike other tools, we do not upload your file to a server. The password is applied locally on your computer, making it impossible for anyone (including us) to intercept your file.

Which encryption method is used?

We use standard 128-bit AES encryption, which is compatible with all major PDF viewers including Adobe Acrobat, Chrome, and Edge.

Can I recover a lost password?

No. Because we don't store your password or file, if you lose the password, the file cannot be recovered by us. Please remember your password!
