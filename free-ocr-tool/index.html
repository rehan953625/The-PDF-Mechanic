What is "Client-Side" OCR?

Client-side technology (WebAssembly) allows your web browser (Chrome, Edge, Safari) to do the heavy lifting. Instead of sending your file to a cloud server, the OCR engine downloads once and runs locally on your laptop or phone. This means zero data leaves your device.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Does this work on Mobile?

Yes! Since it runs in the browser, you can use it on Android (Chrome) and iPhone (Safari) without installing any heavy apps.

What languages are supported?

Currently, the tool works best with English. We are working on adding support for Hindi, Spanish, and French in the next update.

Why is the text sometimes not 100% accurate?

OCR depends heavily on image quality. If the image is blurry, low-resolution, or has bad lighting, the engine might struggle. For best results, scan documents at 300 DPI or ensure good lighting for photos.
