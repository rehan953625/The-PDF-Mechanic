Frequently Asked Questions

Why convert PDF to DOCX offline?

Privacy is the main reason. When you use online converters, you upload your file to their server. With The PDF Mechanic, everything happens on your own computer. It is safer for bank statements and legal papers.

Can I edit the file after conversion?

Yes! The output is a standard Microsoft Word (.docx) file. You can open it in MS Word, Google Docs, or LibreOffice and edit the text completely.

Does it support scanned PDFs?

Our offline tool works best with standard PDFs (created from digital files). For scanned images inside PDFs, you might need OCR software, but our tool ensures text extraction from regular PDFs is accurate.
