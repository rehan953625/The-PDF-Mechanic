Permanently Rotate PDF Pages Offline

Scanned documents often end up in the wrong orientation. Reading upside-down or sideways pages is frustrating. The PDF Mechanic offers a free online PDF rotator that lets you fix the orientation of your documents permanently. Unlike simple viewers that only rotate the view temporarily, our tool saves the new angle to the file itself.

Fix Upside Down PDFs Securely

Many online tools require you to upload your files to a server to rotate them. This is not safe for sensitive documents like contracts or ID proofs. Our tool uses Client-side PDF processing. This means the rotation happens directly in your web browser (Chrome, Safari, Edge). Your files never leave your computer or phone, ensuring 100% privacy.

Rotate PDF on Android & iPhone

You don't need to install an app to fix a sideways PDF. Our tool is optimized for mobile touchscreens. Simply open the website, select your file, and tap the rotate buttons. You can correct mixed orientations (e.g., page 1 is landscape, page 2 is portrait) easily by rotating pages individually. Once done, download the fixed PDF instantly.

Quick Tip

If you scanned a large document completely upside down, use the "Rotate All" buttons at the top to fix the entire file in one click, rather than rotating each page one by one.
