Split PDF Files Offline: Extract Pages Securely

Large PDF documents often contain unnecessary pages. Whether you need to extract a single invoice from a monthly statement or separate a specific chapter from a book, the ability to split PDF files is essential. The PDF Mechanic offers a fast, visual, and secure way to cut PDF documents directly in your browser without uploading them.

Why Split PDF Client-Side?

Privacy is the biggest advantage of using a client-side tool. When you use online splitters that require uploads, you risk exposing sensitive data. Our tool processes the file on your device using JavaScript. This makes it the perfect solution for extracting pages from bank statements, legal contracts, or medical records where confidentiality is critical.

Separate PDF Pages on Android & iOS

Our tool is fully responsive and works like a lightweight app on mobile devices. You don't need to install heavy applications like Adobe Acrobat just to extract a few pages. Simply open The PDF Mechanic on Chrome (Android) or Safari (iOS), select your file, tap the pages you want to keep, and download the new file instantly. It's the fastest way to save specific PDF pages on the go.

Pro Tip

Use the "Select All" button to quickly select everything, then deselect the few pages you don't want. This is faster if you only want to remove one or two pages from a large document.
