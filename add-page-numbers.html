Why use this tool?

Organizing long documents is impossible without page numbers. Whether it's a thesis, a legal contract, or a business report, page numbers provide a professional touch and easier navigation.

The PDF Mechanic processes your file locally. This means you don't have to wait for uploads, and your sensitive documents are never stored on our servers.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is it free to add page numbers?

Yes, completely free. No hidden charges or subscriptions.

Can I format the text as "Page 1 of 5"?

Yes! You can choose from simple numbers ("1"), labeled numbers ("Page 1"), or total count formats ("1 of N").

Does it work on Mac and Windows?

Yes, it works on any operating system (Windows, Mac, Linux) and even on mobile phones, directly in the browser.

Will it remove existing content?

No. The tool overlays the page number on top of your existing content. It does not delete anything.
