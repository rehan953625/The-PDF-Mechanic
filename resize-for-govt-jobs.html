Common Mistake

Don't make the image too small (e.g., under 10KB). If the photo is blurry, your application might be rejected during document verification. Always keep it above the minimum limit.

Troubleshooting: Why is my photo rejected?

  • Wrong Dimensions: Even if size is 20kb, if the aspect ratio isn't 3.5:4.5, it might fail. Crop your photo before compressing.
  • Format Issue: Some phones save as HEIC or PNG. The portals usually only accept JPG/JPEG. Our tool automatically converts everything to JPG.
